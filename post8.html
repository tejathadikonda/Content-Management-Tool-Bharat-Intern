Jessica Jones, a former superhero with superhuman strength and a private investigator at Alias Investigations, navigates a gritty and complex world.

About Jessica Jones

Superhuman Strength: Jessica possesses superhuman strength, allowing her to lift heavy objects and overpower adversaries with ease.

Private Investigator: After retiring from superhero life, Jessica becomes a private investigator at Alias Investigations, specializing in cases involving people with extraordinary abilities.

Traumatic Past: Jessica has a complex and traumatic past, including a period of mind control by the villainous Kilgrave. She overcomes her struggles and uses her experiences to help others.

Alias Investigations: Jessica runs Alias Investigations, her own detective agency, where she takes on cases that often lead her into the darker corners of the Marvel Universe.

Defender: Jessica is a member of the Defenders, a team of street-level heroes that includes Daredevil, Luke Cage, and Iron Fist. Together, they protect New York City from various threats.
