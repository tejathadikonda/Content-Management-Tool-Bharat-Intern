Facts you should know about The Hulk

The Hulk, alter ego of Dr. Bruce Banner, is a Marvel superhero known for his incredible strength and uncontrollable, gamma-ray-induced transformations.

About The Hulk

Gamma Radiation: Dr. Bruce Banner, the man behind The Hulk, gained his incredible powers through exposure to gamma radiation. When angered or stressed, he undergoes a transformation into the massive, green-skinned Hulk.

Unrivaled Strength: The Hulk possesses superhuman strength, making him one of the most powerful beings in the Marvel Universe. His strength increases proportionally with his level of anger.

Uncontrollable Rage: The Hulk's transformations are triggered by intense emotions, particularly anger. While he is a heroic figure, his inability to control his rage makes him a formidable force on the battlefield.

Scientist and Genius: Despite his monstrous appearance, Bruce Banner is a highly intelligent scientist with expertise in multiple fields. The Hulk's brilliance complements his raw power.

Avenger and Defender: The Hulk has been a member of the Avengers and has defended Earth against formidable threats. His gamma-charged abilities make him an asset in the battle against powerful adversaries.
