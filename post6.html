Doctor Stephen Strange, known as Doctor Strange, is a Marvel superhero and the Sorcerer Supreme, wielding mystical arts and protecting the Earth from supernatural threats.

About Doctor Strange

Sorcerer Supreme: Dr. Stephen Strange is the Sorcerer Supreme, a title bestowed upon the most powerful sorcerer tasked with defending the Earth from mystical and supernatural threats.

Mystical Arts Mastery: Doctor Strange is a master of the mystic arts, wielding spells, enchantments, and magical artifacts to manipulate reality, time, and dimensions.

Former Surgeon turned Sorcerer: Before becoming a sorcerer, Stephen Strange was a brilliant and arrogant neurosurgeon. A car accident led him on a journey of spiritual discovery, eventually leading him to the mystic arts.

Eye of Agamotto: Doctor Strange possesses the Eye of Agamotto, a powerful mystical artifact that allows him to see through illusions, manipulate time, and protect against supernatural threats.

Member of the Avengers: Doctor Strange is a crucial member of the Avengers, contributing his unique magical abilities to the team's arsenal in the battle against cosmic and supernatural adversaries.
